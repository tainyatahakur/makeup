At Pryanka Glamour World, we offer a range of professional makeup services to enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and glamorous for any occasion.

Our skilled makeup artists use high-quality products and techniques to create stunning looks for bridal, special occasion, and photo shoot makeup. Let us help you look and feel your best.

Whether you're looking for a natural, subtle look or a bold, dramatic style, our makeup artists will work with you to create the perfect look to match your personality and preferences.

Our bridal makeup services are designed to make you feel confident and radiant on your special day, and we offer a variety of packages to suit your needs and budget.

MAKEUP ARTIST

Pryanka Verma

Pryanka has a passion for makeup and hairstyling that has kept her dedicated to perfecting her craft for over a decade. Her love for the industry shines through in her work, and her clients trust her expertise and creativity.

Pryanka's dedication to makeup and hairstyling has driven her to constantly seek out new techniques and trends, allowing her to provide her clients with the latest styles and looks. Her years of experience have made her a skilled and versatile artist, able to create both natural and dramatic makeup and hairstyles for any occasion. She takes pride in her work and enjoys helping her clients feel confident and beautiful.
